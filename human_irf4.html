The IRF4 gene and autoimmunity in humans

Gene: IRF4

IRF4 is an imporant transcription factor that can drive autoimmune disease when its sequence is altered by small mutations. Linking the exact type of mutation to disease phenotype will help us tailor specific treatments for individuals based on their precise genetic makeup.
