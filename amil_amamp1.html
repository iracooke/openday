Antimicrobial Peptide AmAMP1

Gene: AmAMP1

The AmAMP1 gene encodes an antimicrobial peptide. This peptide selectively inhibits certain types of bacteria. By producing this peptide the coral could potentially control which species of bacteria populate its microbiome

Branching coral

JCU researcher Prof. David Miller and his team sequenced the genome of Acropora millepora and discovered the AmAMP1 gene. This gene is thought to be involved in controlling the coral microbiome, which is an essential component of coral health
