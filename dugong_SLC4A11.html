The SLC4A11 gene in Dugongs

Gene: SLC4A11

The SLC4A11 gene is involved in osmoregulation. Whales and dugongs both have modified versions of this gene that allow them to live in seawater

Dugongs are marine mammals that graze on seagrass. Around the world Dugongs are threatened by habitat loss and hunting.

PhD student Eva Paulus is sequencing the genomes of hundreds of dugongs to assess their movement and population status across northern Australia
